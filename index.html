How it works

01

Pick your target

Choose a window or tab. Lense records it silently in the background while you work naturally.

02

Switch back to zoom

Come back to Lense any time. Hold Shift and drag on the preview to zoom into any region of your recording.

03

Zoom out, keep going

Press Esc or click Zoom Out. Switch back to your target and keep working — recording continues.

04

Stop and save

Click Stop. Your video downloads instantly, with webcam and all zooms baked in.
